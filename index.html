WE ARE ON A MISSION

Creating Access. Inspiring Action. Changing Stories.

At Tech with Khalid Foundation (Twik), we believe talent is everywhere but opportunity isn’t. That’s why we exist: to make digital access and tech education available to every young person, no matter their background or story. We go beyond coding and gadgets—our programs are built to spark confidence, leadership, and real-world impact in a tech-driven generation.

  • We teach practical digital skills that unlock real opportunities.
  • We shape mindsets that think bigger, act smarter, and lead with purpose.
  • From classrooms to careers, we help youth access jobs, scholarships, and global exposure.
  • We collaborate with schools, organizations, and local leaders to drive change from the ground up.
  • We nurture creativity, problem-solving, and the courage to build something new.

How We Create Real Transformation

We meet young people where they are, and equip them to grow, lead, and create in a digital world.

Onboarding Stage

The journey starts here. Building digital confidence from the ground up. We introduce young minds to the tools, mindset, and habits that make thriving in the digital world possible.

  • STEM literacy made simple
  • Internet safety & responsible digital use
  • Collaborating through modern platforms
  • Creating a personal digital portfolio
  • Confidence and growth mindset development

Development Stage

Here’s where things get real. Participants dive into creative, technical, and leadership experiences designed to shape professionals, innovators, and entrepreneurs ready for the modern world.

  • Creative & design thinking
  • Practical technical skills
  • Leadership & team collaboration
  • Social intelligence and networking
