Free tech education, built for you.

TWiK gives under-served teens practical tech skills, 1-on-1 mentorship, and a clear path to real career opportunities — completely free. Whether you're brand new to tech or already curious, you belong here.

Every contribution goes directly to student access — devices, data, school fees and outreach. No amount is too small.

₦5,000 Helps a student stay connected to online classes using a shared phone
₦10,000 Covers learning materials, data, and access for one student during a training cycle
₦30,000 Contributes to a shared laptop students use to learn, practice, and build projects
